About Matimbwa Secondary School

Our History

Matimbwa Secondary School was established in 1985 as a government institution to provide quality secondary education to the residents of Makueni County, Kenya. Over the past four decades, the school has grown from a modest start with barely 50 students to a thriving institution serving over 850 students across all streams. The school's commitment to academic excellence combined with holistic development has made it a beacon of hope in the region.

The school's name "Matimbwa" is derived from the Kamba word meaning "place of learning," which perfectly encapsulates our mission to be a center of academic and personal growth for all our students.

Our Mission

To provide quality, accessible, and inclusive education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values to become responsible citizens and productive members of society, ready to face the challenges of the 21st century.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence, innovation, and character development that produces globally competitive students who contribute positively to national development.
