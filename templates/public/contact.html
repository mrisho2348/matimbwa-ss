â“ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the admission process?

Students are admitted through the national selection process. Submit your Form 4 exam results and follow the KNEC instructions. Contact our office for specific guidelines.

Are scholarships available?

Yes, we offer scholarships for needy but academically talented students. Contact the Principal's office for scholarship application details and requirements.

When does the school year begin?

The school operates on a three-term system. First term begins in January, second term in April, and third term in July. Check our news section for exact dates.

What are the school fees?

School fees vary by form level and stream. Installment plans are available for needy students. Contact the Accounts office for detailed fee schedules.

Can I visit the school?

Yes, we welcome visits during school hours (8:00 AM - 4:00 PM). Please call ahead to arrange a campus tour or meeting with the Principal.

What facilities are available for boarding?

We have modern dormitories with basic amenities. Boarding is optional. Interested students should indicate this during admission.
